WELCOME TO THE 70' !

Mastermind is a code-breaking game for two players.
Here, you'll be playing against the machine.

  1. The rules are :
  2. Start from the bottom line of the board.
  3. Select a peg and guess it's place in the line.
  4. Once you guessed the 4 possibilities, hit "SUBMIT".
  5. The game gives you hints located on the side box of the board.
  6. A black peg means a correct guess (color and position).
  7. A white peg means a correct color but in the wrong position.
  8. You only have 10 tries or the machine wins !
