Mastermind is a code-breaking game for two players.
Here, you'll be playing against the machine.

  • The rules are :
  • Start from the bottom line of the board.
  • Select a peg and guess it's place in the line.
  • Once you guessed the 4 possibilities, hit "SUBMIT".
  • The game gives you hints located on the side box of the board.
  • A black peg means a correct guess (color and position).
  • A white peg means a correct color but in the wrong position.
  • You only have 10 tries or the machine wins !
